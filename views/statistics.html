EBOLA STATISTICS

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), as of 22 November 2022, there have been 141 confirmed cases of Ebola virus disease (EVD), including 55 deaths (case fatality rate: 39%). No new cases have been reported in week 47. Among these, at least 19 healthcare workers were infected, of whom seven died.
